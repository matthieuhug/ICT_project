What is an eSIM?

An eSIM (embedded SIM) is a digital SIM that allows you to activate a cellular plan from your carrier without using a physical SIM card. eSIMs are especially useful for people who travel frequently, as they can switch carriers or plans quickly.

Steps to Set Up an eSIM

  1. Check if your device supports eSIM technology. Most modern smartphones, tablets, and some smartwatches do.
  2. Contact your carrier to get an eSIM activation code, often in the form of a QR code or app-based configuration.
  3. On your device, go to the settings menu and find the section for cellular or network settings.
  4. Select "Add Cellular Plan" or "Add eSIM" and scan the QR code provided by your carrier or input the activation details.
  5. Wait for the activation to complete. Once done, you should see the new plan in your device's cellular settings.
